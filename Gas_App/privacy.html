Information We Collect

1. Fuel & Expense Entries

  • What: Date, station/location description, odometer reading, fuel quantity, unit price, total cost, payment type, notes, associated vehicle.
  • Why: To calculate MPG, cost-per-mile, and analytics.
  • Where stored: Locally on your device and in Supabase only if you enable sync.

2. Payments & Cash Flow

  • What: Payment date, amount, source (e.g., reimbursement, corporate card), type, and notes.
  • Why: To reconcile expenses versus payments and display balances.
  • Where stored: Locally/offline by default; synced when cloud backup is enabled.

3. Vehicles & Maintenance

  • What: Vehicle make/model/year, VIN (optional), license plate, mileage, service intervals, maintenance history, preferred fuel type.
  • Why: To show per-vehicle analytics, reminders, and service history.

4. Profile & Settings (Optional)

  • What: Display name, email, avatar URL, notification settings, preferred units/currency/theme, sync settings.
  • Why: To personalize the App and allow multi-device sync.

5. Diagnostics & Analytics (Optional)

  • What: Screen usage, anonymous identifiers, device model, OS version, app version, crash logs.
  • Why: To fix bugs, measure performance, and guide product improvements.
  • Control: Disable analytics anytime in Settings ‚Üí Privacy.

  • Why: To respond to inquiries and resolve issues.

Data Storage, Security & Retention

Local Storage

All logs, vehicles, and settings are stored in encrypted device storage (AsyncStorage). Clearing data or uninstalling removes this information.

Cloud Sync (Optional)

When enabled, data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and stored in Supabase projects hosted in ISO-certified data centers with row-level security rules to isolate each user.

Retention

  • Local data persists until you delete it.
  • Synced data is retained until you delete your Supabase profile or request deletion.
  • Diagnostics are kept up to 24 months.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Authentication, database, file storage. Privacy Policy.
  • Expo / Apple / Google: Required for push notifications, updates, and crash reporting at the OS level.
  • Email Providers: We use standard email services (e.g., Gmail/Outlook) to respond to support requests.

No advertising SDKs or third-party analytics networks are embedded in Gas Tracker.
